About Dr. Vinita Sharma

Dr. Vinita Sharma is the Founder and Director of DermaGLO Laser Skin Clinic, Bengaluru, with over 17 years of experience in medical aesthetics, lasers, injectables, hair restoration, and non-surgical facial rejuvenation.

A respected name in aesthetic medicine, she is an International Speaker at AMWC and iSWAM and has trained with globally renowned experts in Botox, dermal fillers, and facial aesthetics. Her approach combines medical precision, artistic judgment, and a deep understanding of facial anatomy to deliver results that are safe, natural-looking, and individually tailored.

FKCCI Award Winner & selected for the prestigious Goldman Sachs 10,000 Women Entrepreneurs Program at IIM Bangalore — reflecting her commitment to clinical excellence, leadership, and ethical aesthetic medicine.
